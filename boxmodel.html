The 41-year-old will script history for the umpteenth time. MS Dhoni is the most capped player in the Indian Premier League and will become the first player to lead a franchise for the 200th time

Dhoni as captain in IPL The former Indian skipper has led CSK to the playoff in 11 out of 13 seasons of IPL. Dhoni has featured in 213 matches as the captain of an IPL team. Under MS Dhoni, CSK has reached the final a record 9 times, winning the championship four times.
